About Me

I’m a software engineer from Vancouver, Canada. I graduated with an honours degree in computer science from the University of British Columbia, and am currently working at Google Kirkland on the Asylo enclave framework and other confidential computing efforts.

In 2019, I interned at Riot Games in Los Angeles, where I built a UI in React that provided a quick overview of the configuration state of any microservice in the fleet. This allowed rollouts of game patches to be made with a higher degree of confidence.

The summer before that, I interned at Google HQ on an internal team that triages and curates feedback and bug reports for other various Google products. I worked on modifications to the app’s Java backend to implement auditable changelogs.

My first internship was in 2017, where I worked at Axiom Zen in downtown Vancouver. I had the opportunity to work on several projects while there, ranging from machine learning experiments to full-stack development on new products.

Feel free to get in touch via email or Twitter!
